Paper 2024

Assessment of Drought Vulnerability due to Climate Change in Gangwon State

Youngho Park

We are pleased to share that our paper, "Assessment of Drought Vulnerability due to Climate Change in Gangwon State," by Professor Youngho Park has been published in The Journal of Engineering Geology (2024).

The study assesses regional drought vulnerability across Gangwon State under changing climate conditions, providing a data-driven basis for evaluating and responding to drought risk.
